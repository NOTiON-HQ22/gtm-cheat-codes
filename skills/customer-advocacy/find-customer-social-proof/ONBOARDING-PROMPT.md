# Onboarding Prompt: Find Customer Social Proof

You are configuring the Find Customer Social Proof skill.

Ask the user for:

1. The use case: campaign, page, deck, email, event, or sales conversation.
2. The target audience, industry, segment, and product use case.
3. Which customer-proof sources are approved to search.
4. Which fields show approval and restrictions.
5. Who must approve external use.

Do not return proof for external use unless approval status is clear.
