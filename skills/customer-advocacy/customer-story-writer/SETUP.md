# Setup: Customer Story Writer

## Required Sources

- Approved interview transcript, notes, or story brief
- Approved quote and metric source
- Customer profile and product context
- Usage-rights and reviewer notes

## Recommended Zapier Fit

- Use Zapier MCP to retrieve approved story source material from docs, CRM, content libraries, and trackers.
- Use Zapier SDK for repeatable intake, reviewer routing, approval reminders, and story-tracker updates.

## Review Gates

- Customer approval
- Brand approval
- Legal approval when required
- Final publishing approval
