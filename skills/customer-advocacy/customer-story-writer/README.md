# Customer Story Writer

## Use When

Use this when you have a customer interview, transcript, notes, or approved proof points and need a story draft that is specific, sourced, and ready for human review.

## What It Does

It turns raw customer material into either a narrative blog or campaign story, or a metric-forward customer story page. It pulls out the person, problem, workflow, impact, exact quotes, verified metrics, and approval gaps.

## Inputs

- Customer interview transcript, notes, or brief
- Intended story format
- Approved quotes and metrics
- Customer profile and product context
- Usage rights and reviewer requirements

## Outputs

- Draft customer story
- Quote and metric checklist
- Open questions
- Approval gaps
- Optional alternate headline and angle options

## Setup

1. Connect approved transcript, document, CRM, and content-library sources.
2. Map the fields in `SCHEMA-MAP.md`.
3. Run `ONBOARDING-PROMPT.md` before the first real story draft.
4. Keep customer, brand, and legal review steps in place.

## Safety

Do not invent quotes, metrics, customer approvals, or outcomes. Do not publish customer names, logos, quotes, or stats unless they are approved for that exact use.
