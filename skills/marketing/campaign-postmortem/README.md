# Campaign Postmortem

## Use When

Use this when a campaign ended, missed target, exceeded expectations, or created lessons that are scattered across docs, dashboards, meetings, and chat.

## What It Does

It builds a concise, sourced retro: what happened, what worked, what broke, what changed, and what the team should repeat, change, or stop next time.

## Inputs

- Campaign name, goal, and date range
- Launch brief and planning docs
- Channel and funnel performance
- CRM, pipeline, revenue, or conversion results
- Team feedback and timeline notes
- Follow-up actions or unresolved questions

## Outputs

- Executive summary
- Timeline
- Goal vs. actuals
- What worked
- What did not work
- Root causes
- Repeat / change / stop recommendations
- Action register

## Setup

1. Connect approved docs, analytics, CRM, meeting notes, and project trackers.
2. Map the fields in `SCHEMA-MAP.md`.
3. Run `ONBOARDING-PROMPT.md` before the first retro.
4. Decide which metrics and business claims require review before sharing.

## Safety

Do not invent metrics or causes. Cite sources, label unknowns, and keep the tone direct without blaming individuals.
